Unique Token Frameworks

Specialized systems guiding token development and usage

Strategic Flexibility

Mathematical rigor with pragmatic exceptions. Spacing tokens like space075 (6px) provide flexibility while tracking ≥80% appropriate usage to prevent abuse.

Compositional Architecture

Typography tokens separate concerns by property type. Color applied separately from typography, enabling flexible combinations without duplication.

Lighting Framework

Shadow offsets follow sun arc (sunrise → noon → sunset) with art theory-based colors. Warm light creates cool shadows, cool light creates warm shadows.

Relationship Hierarchy

Semantic spacing based on element relationships (grouped, related, separated, sectioned) rather than arbitrary values. Communicates intent, not just size.

Platform-Specific Conventions

Layering tokens respect platform differences: web/iOS separate z-index from shadows, Android couples elevation with shadows per Material Design.

Vertical Rhythm Alignment

Line height tokens use precision multipliers to maintain 8pt vertical rhythm when paired with fontSize tokens, ensuring consistent baseline grid.
